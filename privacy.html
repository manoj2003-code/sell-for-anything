Our Commitment to Privacy

At Sell For Anything, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment information when you use our services or place an order.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us process orders, provide customer support, improve our services, send updates, and prevent fraud. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with trusted partners like payment processors and delivery services. These partners are obligated to keep your data secure.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we strive to keep your personal information safe.

5. Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your personal information by logging into your account. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.
